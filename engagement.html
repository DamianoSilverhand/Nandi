Community Participation In National Development

Promoting selfless activities that need to be performed in communities with a view to touching lives is one of our primary goals. We are working with partners and our hub networks to connect young people and support them to channel their passion into lobbying, rallying, awareness raising and meeting with decision makers so that they make an impact at local and national level.

Our aim is develop sustainable Social Enterprises that are run by, and benefit the local people

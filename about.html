Raising A Value Added Youth

Nandi Youth Foundation is formed by groups of Zambian citizens who are ready to help lift the nation in a holistic manner without leaving any stone unturned in both urban and rural settings.With more emphasis on the rural areas which are far from opportunities and privileges, however patriotism and citizenry is the driving force with a view of cultivating a value added youth.

Our Vision And Aim

-To inculcate a high level of patriotism in all youths that will be expressed in hard work
-To promote talents,skills,dreams and a zeal for the youth to participate and contribute to national development.
-To promote self-reliance among group members after completion of each project season.
-To enhance behaviour change through capacity building and exposure.

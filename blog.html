Ignite Zambia

April 1, 2018 by Damiano

Nandi Youth Foundation soon to launch Innovation Competition.

This is an empowerment program for youths in Zambia; it was established by Nandi Youth Foundation as a way to keep in touch with the youths as they work towards championing the issue of youth poverty and unemployment. The intention of this program is to spark hard work, innovations, creativity, initiative and creating employment opportunities for youths.this program will bring together youths of substance together from all walks of life, to be trained as advocates and human rights experts, in order for them to go back to their communities and implement what they have learnt. This will help reduce on a number of unemployed youths in Zambia; it will also help the communities face challenges related to human rights and in a right manner.
